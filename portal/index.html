Nwestco Website Redesign

Design Directions

Prototype Options

Three distinct approaches to Nwestco's new website, each optimized for different user journeys and business goals.

Option A: One-Page Impact

Fully Built

Single-page design with interactive widgets. All content condensed into one scrollable experience with flip boxes, carousels, tabs, and modals.

  • Single scrolling page with all content
  • Flip box widgets for services (7 cards)
  • Filterable projects portfolio (6 projects)
  • Testimonial carousel with 5 reviews
  • Tabbed market sub-segments
  • Expandable location cards (9 branches)
  • Legal content in modals
  • Touch-friendly & accessible
View Option A

Option B: Traditional Multi-Page

Fully Built

Comprehensive multi-page website with complete content across all sections. Full service pages, market pages, locations, and contact functionality.

  • 20+ fully built pages with content
  • Complete navigation structure
  • 7 detailed service pages
  • 3 market vertical pages
  • 9 location pages
  • Interactive service timeline
  • Contact forms & CTAs
View Option B

Option C: Modern Progressive

Fully Built

Modern progressive web app approach with fullscreen navigation, video hero backgrounds, and cinematic design language.

  • 20+ fully built pages with content
  • Fullscreen immersive navigation
  • Video hero backgrounds
  • 3 market vertical pages
  • 7 detailed service pages
  • Careers, locations, projects
  • Legal pages (Privacy, Terms, Accessibility)
  • Modern animations & transitions
View Option C
