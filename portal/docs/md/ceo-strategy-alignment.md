# CEO Strategy Prototype Alignment

This document confirms how the CEO's M&A Strategy deck content has been incorporated into all 3 website prototype options.

---

## Source Document
**Marketing Excerpts (M&A Strategy Deck)** - Internal strategy presentation outlining Nwestco's core values, value proposition, target markets, and growth vision.

---

## Strategic Alignment Matrix

| CEO Deck Element | Deck Content | Prototype Implementation |
|------------------|--------------|--------------------------|
| **Value Proposition** | "A broad-spectrum of turnkey, lifecycle solutions, seamlessly delivered, to maximize the uptime and performance of fuel systems and car wash locations" | Hero messaging: "WE KEEP YOU RUNNING. COMPLIANT. PROFITABLE." - Focuses on uptime and performance outcomes |
| **3 Target Markets** | Fuel Systems (retail, commercial, industrial), Car Washes (tunnels, in-bay, self-serve), Environmental (related to petroleum) | Dedicated market pages in navigation: Fuel Systems, Car Wash Systems, Environmental Solutions |
| **Service Lifecycle Wheel** | 7 touchpoints: Installation, Service & Maintenance, Remodels & Upgrades, Equipment Sales, Testing & Compliance, Environmental Solutions, Chemistry | Services menu structure mirrors lifecycle: Design & Engineering, Installation & Construction, 24/7 Service & Maintenance, Remodels & Upgrades, Equipment Sales & Parts, Testing & Compliance, Training & Certification |
| **The 3 Uniques** | 1. Broad lifecycle solutions ("cradle-to-grave") 2. Geography/coverage (entire Western US) 3. Subject Matter/Technical Expertise (best-in-class training) 4. Agility and speed | Dedicated "The 3 Uniques" section on About/Why Nwestco page with detailed explanations of each differentiator |
| **10-Year Target** | Revenue: $500M, EBITDA: $75M (15%) | About page: "$120M company on path to $500M" - Positions current scale while showing growth trajectory |
| **Core Values** | 1. People-first, always 2. Customer-focus 3. Integrity, Respect, Transparency, Honesty 4. Work hard, Be nice, Have fun 5. Best-in-Class | Careers section emphasizes people-first culture; About page references values; Best-in-Class messaging throughout |
| **Core Focus/Purpose** | "To build a great company with great people and great results" / "Help make our customers wildly successful" | Careers messaging focuses on building great teams; Customer success messaging throughout service pages |
| **Mission** | "Be Best-In-Class in everything we do" | "Best-in-class" language used for technicians, training, equipment, and service delivery |
| **Proven Process** | Understand customers' real needs, Data-driven solutions, Flawless execution and "wow" experience | Pain point flip cards demonstrate understanding of customer challenges with data-driven solutions |
| **Guarantee** | "Solutions that maximize customers' uptime and operating performance" | "90%+ of calls completed within 48 hours", "48-hour service guarantee", uptime-focused messaging |
| **Supporting Elements** | Response times, Technical capabilities, Industry-leading brands, Coverage/geography | Stats sections: "2-4 hour average response time", "360+ factory-certified technicians", "9 branches across 6 Western states" |
| **Customer Segmentation** | National/Regional/Local, Retail/Commercial/Industrial, Various verticals (C-Store, Gas Station, Car Wash, etc.) | Market pages address segment-specific needs; Service pages speak to different customer types |

---

## Implementation by Prototype Option

### Option A: One-Page Scrollable
- Service lifecycle wheel represented as scroll sections
- 3 Uniques presented in dedicated scroll section
- Value proposition in hero with supporting stats
- Customer pain points with flip-card solutions

### Option B: Traditional Multi-Page
- Full service pages for each lifecycle touchpoint
- Dedicated market pages for 3 target segments
- Comprehensive About page with 3 Uniques, values, and company stats
- Geographic coverage emphasized with location pages

### Option C: Modern/Bold
- Pain-point focused hero messaging aligned with "maximize uptime" guarantee
- Interactive flip cards showing challenges → solutions (Proven Process)
- "Why Nwestco" page with The 3 Uniques prominently featured
- Stats section highlighting $120M → $500M growth trajectory
