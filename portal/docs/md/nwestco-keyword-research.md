# Nwestco Keyword Research & Opportunities

**Project:** Nwestco Website Redesign
**Research Date:** November 26, 2025
**Researcher:** Technical SEO Specialist
**Target Markets:** Western US (WA, OR, ID, MT, AK + service reach)

---

## Executive Summary

This keyword research identifies 250+ keyword opportunities across Nwestco's service offerings, organized by search intent, geographic targeting, and business value. The strategy focuses on dominating local markets through the 8-branch footprint while establishing regional authority for Western US petroleum and car wash services.

### Priority Keyword Tiers

**Tier 1 - High Value, Achievable (Target First 6 Months):**
- 40 keywords with local intent and moderate competition
- Estimated combined monthly searches: 8,000-12,000
- Focus: [Service] + [City] combinations for 9 branch locations

**Tier 2 - Medium Value, Competitive (Months 6-12):**
- 80 keywords with regional intent and higher competition
- Estimated combined monthly searches: 15,000-20,000
- Focus: Western US regional terms, service lifecycle keywords

**Tier 3 - Long-Tail, Niche (Ongoing Content Strategy):**
- 130+ long-tail keywords with specific intent
- Lower individual volume but high conversion potential
- Focus: Technical specifications, compliance, problem-solving queries

## Keyword Research Methodology

### Data Sources
Due to limitations in accessing proprietary keyword tools during this audit, estimates are based on:
- SERP analysis and competitor research
- Industry knowledge and search trends
- Comparative analysis with similar B2B service industries
- Geographic search volume patterns for multi-location businesses

### Search Volume Indicators
- **High:** 1,000+ monthly searches
- **Medium:** 100-999 monthly searches
- **Low:** 10-99 monthly searches
- **Niche:** <10 monthly searches (high intent)

### Competition Levels
- **Low:** Small local competitors, directory listings
- **Medium:** Regional competitors, industry sites
- **High:** National brands, established authorities
- **Very High:** Major industry players, enterprise sites

### Business Value Scoring (1-10)
- 9-10: Direct service inquiry, high purchase intent, qualified lead
- 7-8: Research phase, service comparison, future opportunity
- 5-6: General information, early awareness, long-term nurture
- 1-4: Informational only, low conversion potential
