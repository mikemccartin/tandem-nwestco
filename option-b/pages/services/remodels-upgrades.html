Remodels & Upgrades

Modernize your facility without complete reconstruction. Strategic equipment upgrades, facility refreshes, and compliance improvements delivered in phases to minimize downtime.

Why Remodels and Upgrades Are Necessary

Aging Equipment Drives Customers to Newer Competitors

Your fuel dispensers look dated compared to the modern station across the street. Your car wash tunnel is functional but shows its age. Customers judge your business by appearance - aging equipment signals lack of investment.

New Regulations Require Equipment Upgrades

EMV payment compliance. LED lighting mandates. Updated leak detection requirements. New environmental regulations. Compliance isn't optional - you need upgrades to meet current codes and avoid fines or forced closure.

Complete Reconstruction is Too Expensive and Disruptive

Closing for months while rebuilding from scratch is financially impossible for most operators. You need phased upgrades that modernize your facility while keeping operations running.

Strategic Remodeling That Maximizes ROI

Nwestco's remodel and upgrade services transform aging fuel stations and car washes into modern, efficient operations through strategic equipment replacement, facility refreshes, and compliance improvements - all delivered in phases that minimize operational disruption.

Equipment Replacement

Fuel dispenser replacement with modern Gilbarco or Wayne models. Car wash equipment upgrades to Ryko, Belanger, or Rotary. Payment system upgrades to EMV-compliant terminals.

Facility Refresh

Canopy painting and structural improvements. Concrete work for drainage and appearance. Signage upgrades. LED canopy lighting conversion for energy savings.

Compliance-Driven Upgrades

EMV payment compliance. Leak detection upgrades for EPA requirements. Environmental equipment for California regulations. Updated electrical systems for current NEC codes.

Phased Implementation

We plan remodel projects in phases that minimize downtime. Replace two dispensers while others remain operational. Schedule major work during lowest-traffic periods.

Why Choose Nwestco for Remodels and Upgrades

Increase Revenue

Updated dispensers with large color displays increase transaction speed. Modern car wash equipment improves wash quality. Fresh appearance attracts new customers and rebuilds traffic.

Phased Approach Minimizes Downtime

We plan remodels to keep operations running throughout construction. Replace dispensers in stages. Schedule car wash equipment upgrades during off-peak seasons.

Meet Compliance Requirements

EMV payment deadlines. Environmental regulations. Fire codes. We ensure upgrades meet all current requirements - protecting you from fines and forced closures.

One Contractor Handles Everything
