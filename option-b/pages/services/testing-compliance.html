Testing & Compliance

Stay compliant with EPA and state fuel system regulations. Tank integrity testing, leak detection certification, and compliance documentation to protect your business from fines and liability.

Why Compliance is Critical and Complex

Compliance Deadlines Create Constant Anxiety

Tank testing due dates, leak detection annual certifications, EPA reporting requirements, state inspection schedules - compliance obligations never end. Miss a deadline and you face fines, potential closure, and environmental liability.

EPA Violations Carry Severe Financial Consequences

EPA fines for underground storage tank violations range from $10,000 to $250,000 or more per violation. State agencies add additional penalties. Environmental cleanup costs can reach millions.

You're Not a Regulatory Expert But You're Held Accountable

EPA regulations. State environmental codes. Local fire marshal requirements. These rules are complex, technical, and constantly changing. You need experts who track regulations and keep you ahead of compliance obligations.

Comprehensive Compliance Services

Nwestco's testing and compliance services protect your business through tank integrity testing, leak detection system certification, EPA and state regulatory compliance, complete documentation for inspections and audits, and proactive guidance on changing requirements.

Tank Integrity Testing

Precision testing to verify underground storage tanks are structurally sound and leak-free. EPA-compliant testing methods. Triennial testing schedules. Complete documentation with certification letters.

Leak Detection Certification

Annual certification of leak detection systems for tanks and piping. Installation and calibration of new equipment. Monthly monitoring reports for automatic systems.

Regulatory Consulting

Expert guidance on EPA underground storage tank regulations (40 CFR Part 280). State-specific requirements across six Western states. Inspection preparation and representation.

Automated Compliance Management

Scheduled testing reminders so you never miss deadlines. Calendar management across multiple locations. Regulatory change alerts. Complete compliance history tracking.
