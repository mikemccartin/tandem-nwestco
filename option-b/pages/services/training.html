Training & Certification

Best-in-class technical training programs for fuel systems and car wash equipment. Hands-on training at our Portland and Spokane facilities, on-site programs, and manufacturer certification courses.

Why Technical Training Matters

Untrained Staff Create Operational Problems and Safety Risks

Employees who don't understand fuel system safety procedures create regulatory violations and liability exposure. Technicians without proper training troubleshoot by trial-and-error, causing equipment damage.

Manufacturer Training Requires Travel and Time Away

Sending employees to manufacturer training facilities means flights, hotels, and days away from operations. Small operators can't afford the time and expense. You need accessible, local training.

Generic Training Doesn't Address Your Specific Equipment

Online courses and generic safety training don't teach troubleshooting on your Gilbarco dispensers or maintenance on your Ryko tunnel system. You need hands-on training on your actual equipment.

Comprehensive Technical Training Programs

Nwestco's Portland and Spokane training centers deliver hands-on technical education for fuel system and car wash operators, technicians, and managers - combining classroom instruction with practical exercises on actual equipment.

Portland & Spokane Facilities

State-of-the-art training centers with working fuel dispensers, underground tank systems, leak detection equipment, car wash systems, and payment terminals. Learn on real equipment.

Flexible Formats

Public classes with open enrollment. Private sessions exclusively for your team. On-site training at your facility. Virtual training options for applicable topics.

Expert Instructors

Our trainers are experienced field technicians who've diagnosed thousands of real-world problems - not just classroom instructors reading manuals.

Manufacturer Certifications

Certification programs from Gilbarco, Veeder-Root, Ryko, Belanger, and other manufacturers. Certifications protect warranties and demonstrate competence.

Training Course Categories

Fuel System Training

Dispenser operation and maintenance, underground tank systems, leak detection, compliance, electrical systems for petroleum facilities, NEC requirements for hazardous locations.

Car Wash Training

Tunnel system operation, in-bay automatic service, chemistry application, equipment maintenance, troubleshooting common issues, chemical injection systems.

Safety & Compliance Training

EPA regulations, hazmat handling, emergency procedures, safety protocols, documentation requirements, spill prevention and response.

Management & Operations

Preventative maintenance programs, inventory management, staff development, operational optimization, equipment lifecycle management.
