Design & Engineering

Expert design and permitting for faster project approvals. CAD drawings, site planning, compliance consulting, and permitting assistance from engineers with 25+ years of regulatory expertise.

Why Design and Permitting Expertise Matters

Permit Rejections Delay Your Revenue Timeline

Every week your project waits for permit approval is a week without revenue. Design mistakes mean rejected permits, costly rework, and months of delays. You need drawings and specifications that pass inspection the first time, not multiple resubmissions that push back your opening date.

Regulatory Requirements Are Complex and Unforgiving

EPA fuel system regulations, state environmental codes, local fire marshal requirements, building codes, electrical standards - compliance is mandatory but confusing. One overlooked requirement can halt your entire project or trigger expensive mid-construction changes that blow your budget.

Generic Contractors Don't Understand Fuel and Car Wash Regulations

General contractors and architects know buildings, but fuel dispensing systems and car wash facilities have specialized regulatory requirements. You need engineers who've designed hundreds of fuel systems and car washes - experts who know exactly what every jurisdiction requires.

Design Engineering That Accelerates Project Approvals

Nwestco's design and engineering team brings 25+ years of regulatory expertise to fuel system and car wash projects across the Western United States. We create compliant designs, handle permitting coordination, and guide you through approval processes in six Western states.

Complete Design Services

CAD drawings and site planning that meet all regulatory requirements. Fuel dispenser layouts optimized for traffic flow. Tank system design with proper capacity, containment, and monitoring. Electrical system specifications for power distribution and control systems.

Permitting & Compliance Consulting

We navigate the permitting maze so you don't have to. EPA underground storage tank permits. State environmental agency approvals. Local fire marshal and building department coordination. Specialized permits for aviation fuel systems.

Construction Support

Unlike design firms that disappear after drawings are complete, Nwestco provides construction support to ensure the project is built according to plans. We answer inspector questions, handle permit modifications, and ensure final inspections pass smoothly.
