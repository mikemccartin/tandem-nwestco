THE CHALLENGE

Why Environmental Compliance Demands Expert Support

Non-Compliance Means Fines, Shutdowns, and Liability

EPA violations can mean $10,000+ daily fines. State agencies can shut down operations for failed tank tests. Unreported releases create massive liability exposure. You can't afford reactive compliance - you need proactive management from experts who know the regulations.

Regulations Vary Significantly Across States

California requirements differ from Oregon. Montana has different timelines than Washington. Multi-state operators face a patchwork of regulations with different testing frequencies, reporting requirements, and enforcement approaches. You need a partner who knows each state's specific requirements.

Contamination Problems Escalate Quickly and Expensively

A small release ignored becomes a million-dollar remediation project. What starts as a failed tank test can evolve into years of groundwater monitoring. Early detection and proper response prevents small problems from becoming major liabilities.

TESTING & COMPLIANCE

Stay Compliant with Proactive Testing Programs

Nwestco's compliance specialists track testing schedules, coordinate with regulatory agencies, and ensure your fuel systems meet all federal and state requirements before inspectors arrive.

Tank Testing

Underground and aboveground storage tank testing using EPA-approved methods. Precision testing that identifies problems before they become releases. Certified documentation for regulatory compliance.

Line Testing

Pressurized line testing for product piping systems. Detection of small leaks that ATG systems may miss. Required testing intervals tracked and scheduled proactively.

Leak Detection Monitoring

ATG system certification, calibration, and monitoring. Interstitial monitoring, vapor monitoring, and groundwater monitoring programs. 24/7 alarm response.

Regulatory Reporting

EPA Tier I and Tier II reporting, state agency notifications, release reporting, and compliance documentation. We handle the paperwork so you maintain compliance.

WHEN PROBLEMS OCCUR

Site Assessment and Remediation Services

When contamination is discovered - through testing, regulatory inspection, or property transaction - Nwestco guides you through assessment, regulatory coordination, and cleanup. Our environmental specialists have remediated hundreds of petroleum-contaminated sites across the Western US.

Phase I & II Assessments

Environmental due diligence for property transactions. Historical records review, site inspection, soil and groundwater sampling, contamination characterization, and risk assessment.

Corrective Action

Regulatory agency coordination for confirmed releases. Corrective action plans, remediation system design, implementation, and monitoring until site closure is achieved.

Soil Remediation

Contaminated soil excavation and disposal. In-situ treatment options. Confirmation sampling and regulatory documentation for closure.

Groundwater Treatment

Groundwater extraction and treatment systems. Monitoring well installation and sampling programs. Long-term monitoring until closure criteria are met.
