Careers at Nwestco

Join a growing company with 8 branches across 6 Western states. We're always looking for talented technicians, project managers, and professionals who want to build their careers with an industry leader.

WHY NWESTCO

Build Your Career with an Industry Leader

Nwestco isn't just growing - we're building a team of the best fuel system, car wash, and environmental professionals in the Western US. We invest in our people with competitive compensation, ongoing training, and real career advancement opportunities.

Competitive Compensation

Industry-leading pay, comprehensive health benefits, 401(k) with company match, paid time off, and performance bonuses. We pay for excellence.

Training & Certification

Factory training at our Portland and Spokane centers plus manufacturer facilities. We invest in your skills with paid certification programs and ongoing education.

Career Growth

Clear advancement paths from technician to lead to supervisor to management. We promote from within and support your long-term career goals.

CAREER AREAS

Opportunities Across the Company

Service Technicians

Field service for fuel dispensers, car wash equipment, environmental systems, and controls. Diagnose problems, perform repairs, and maintain equipment. Factory training provided.

Installation Crews

Install fuel systems, car wash equipment, environmental systems, and underground tanks. Work on diverse projects from retail stations to commercial fleets. Travel throughout the Western US.

Project Management

Coordinate projects from design through completion. Manage timelines, budgets, subcontractors, and customer relationships. Leadership opportunities available.

Engineering & Design

CAD design, site planning, and permitting coordination. Work with customers and installation teams to design compliant, efficient fuel, car wash, and environmental systems.

Sales & Account Management

Build customer relationships and grow territory revenue. Technical sales background preferred. Uncapped commission potential with established customer base.

Operations & Support

Parts coordination, dispatch, administration, and customer service. Keep our branches running smoothly and our customers satisfied.
